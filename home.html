Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about the Lost & Found portal.

❓ How do I report a lost item?
Click on Get Started → Login → Dashboard → Report Lost Item form.
❓ How do I search for a lost item?
Open dashboard and browse the Found Items Catalog.
❓ What happens if I find my lost item?
Contact admin immediately and cancel your lost report.
❓ Is there a fee to recover my item?
Yes, a small handling fee may apply depending on item type.
❓ Is there a reward for returning found items?
Appreciation certificates or rewards may be given by owners.
